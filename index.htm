Deliciousness in every bite!

Every cookie made at the Kevin Chrispy Cookie company is crafted with only the finest ingredients. Butter from grass fed cows. Unbleached flour with the best flavor and textures. Organic sugar only from Kakira. Eggs from chickens that are local. Even our water is special. It comes from the Rwenzori Mountain springs that we hike up to retrieve. Baking is our passion and we take it very seriously.
